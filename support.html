Rules & Guidelines

To ensure a safe, enjoyable, and fair experience for all our users, please adhere to the following rules:

Account Responsibility

You are solely responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account and password. Any activity that occurs under your account is your responsibility.

Accurate Information

Provide accurate, current, and complete information when creating an account or making a purchase. Falsifying information is strictly prohibited.

One Account Per User

Each user is allowed only one account. Multiple accounts per individual are not permitted.

Prohibited Activities

  • Attempting to access another user's account
  • Using the platform for any illegal activities
  • Uploading or transmitting viruses or any other malicious code
  • Spamming or sending unsolicited messages to other users
  • Impersonating another person or entity

Intellectual Property

Respect copyright and trademark laws. Do not use our platform to infringe upon the intellectual property rights of others.

Product Usage

Use the products purchased from our platform in accordance with their intended purpose and any accompanying terms of service.

Payment and Refunds

All payments are to be made through our secure payment system. Refunds are subject to our refund policy, which can be found on our Payments page.

Community Conduct

Treat all members of our community with respect. Harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory behavior of any kind will not be tolerated.

Content Sharing

If our platform allows content sharing, ensure that your content is appropriate, does not violate any laws, and does not infringe on others' rights.

Reporting Violations

If you encounter any violations of these rules, please report them to our support team immediately.

Rule Updates

We reserve the right to update these rules at any time. Regular review of this page is recommended to stay informed of any changes.

Violation of these rules may result in the suspension or termination of your account. We are committed to maintaining a safe and enjoyable environment for all our users, and we appreciate your cooperation in adhering to these guidelines.

Remember, at Your Fancy Shop, we're all about creating a fantastic digital experience. Let's work together to keep it that way!
